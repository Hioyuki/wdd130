Overview

purpose

1: Who are the target customers?

1: Our target customers are adventure enthusiasts and outdoor lovers who seek thrilling experiences and enjoy engaging in water- based activities. They could be individuals or groups looking for an adrenaline rush and a memorable outdoor adventure.

2: Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on).

2: Our audience typically falls within the age range of 18 to 45, with a moderate to above-average financial situation. They are individuals who value experiences over material possessions, have an interest in outdoor activities, and are physically active and adventurous.

3: What do they want?

3: They want exciting and challenging water rafting experiences that provide a sense of adventure and camaraderie. Our audience seeks opportunities to connect with nature, enjoy thrilling rapids, and create lasting memories with friends or fellow adventure seekers.
